About the Game

Welcome to Minefield Garden, a cheerful logic puzzle set among soft hills, clouds, flags, and playful garden characters.

Tap tiles to reveal what is hidden underneath. Number clues tell you how many hazards are nearby. Use logic and deduction to place flags and clear the grid safely.

Perfect for puzzle lovers of all ages, Minefield Garden offers quick sessions, progressive levels, and satisfying brain-teasing challenges.
